Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

A & S Traders (“we”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information we collect

  • Account: first name, last name, email, optional phone number, and password (stored securely, not in plain text).
  • Orders: delivery address, city, phone, items ordered, payment method, and payment screenshots you upload.
  • Technical: browser type and basic usage needed to run the site (e.g. cart stored in your browser).

2. How we use your information

  • Process and deliver your orders.
  • Verify bank-transfer payments.
  • Contact you about order status or delivery.
  • Manage your account and order history when signed in.
  • Improve our shop and prevent fraud or misuse.

3. Storage & security

Order and account data is stored in our secure database (MongoDB). Passwords are hashed before storage. Payment proof images are kept only as long as needed to confirm payment and handle disputes.

4. Sharing with others

We do not sell your personal data. We may share delivery details with courier or delivery staff so your order can reach you. We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Cookies & local storage

The site uses your browser’s local storage to remember your cart and sign-in session. We do not use third-party advertising trackers. Clearing browser data will sign you out and may empty your cart.

6. Your choices

  • Update account details by contacting us or through available account features.
  • Sign out at any time from the account menu.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data by emailing us (subject to legal record-keeping needs for orders).

7. Children

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the site after updates means you accept the revised policy.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: hello@astraders.pk or +92 300 1234567. See also our Terms of Service.
